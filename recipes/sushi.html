Sushi Rolls can be filled with any ingredients you choose. Try smoked salmon instead of imitation crabmeat. Serve with teriyaki sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups of water
  • 2/3 cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 1/2 pund imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 avocado peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger

Steps:

  1. Step: Preheat the oven to 150 degrees C
  2. Step: Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Step: Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Gently stir into cooked rice in the pot and set aside.
  4. Step: Lay nori sheets on a baking sheet.
  5. Step: Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Step: Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat. Use wet hands to spread a thing layer of rice on top. Arrange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avocado, and pickled gigner over rice in a line down the center. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  7. Step: Use a wet, shart knife to cut each roll into 4 to 6 slices.
