Easy Guacamole recipe

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips of as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients

  • 12 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Step: Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Step: Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
